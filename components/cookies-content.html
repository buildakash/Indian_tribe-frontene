What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to help our site remember you and your preferences.

Why do we use cookies?

  • To maintain cart and session information
  • For performance and analytics
  • For personalization and marketing

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features of our website may not function properly without them.
